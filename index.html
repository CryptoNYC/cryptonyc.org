#blockchain

Crypto NYC is a community, coworking space and app studio based in downtown Brooklyn, dedicated fulltime to blockchain technologies including Bitcoin and Ethereum. Our focus is on people first and technology second. We are a hardworking group of engineers, designers, and schemers passionate about the blockchain and excited about its potential. If you're interested in joining us, drop us a line at hello@cryptonyc.org or apply now!

#where

Located in Brooklyn, right off the A, C, F, and G lines.
